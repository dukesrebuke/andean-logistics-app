Project Andean Logistics provides nearshore dispatch, tracking, and broker communication services to U.S. freight operators from Medellín, Colombia. Operating during full U.S. business hours with transparent per-call billing, we deliver U.S.-grade operational performance at nearshore cost levels. Designed to reach cash-flow positivity at 5-6 agents, with expansion driven by disciplined reinvestment.
